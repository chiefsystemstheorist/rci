\[1\] leave 100s of tabs open for days/ weeks.

It is about gathering the relevant data in the sea of nonsense, and leaving the tabs open, « in working memory », until the gathered data
// All the tabs that are open are not open because people forget to close them. Instead, they act like living bookmarks. They are keys to thoughts and ideas, that you want to return to and continue thinking.

Web pages that we visit and leave open are artifacts of externalized cognition; keys to thinking and remembering. The browser of today is a transitory space that brings us into a mental state, not just to a specific website destination. And we should design our browsers for this task.
